# Welcome to Create: Harmony’s Wiki

**Create: Harmony** is a progression-focused Minecraft modpack built around balance, automation, and long-term world evolution.

This modpack blends **technical creativity**, **structured progression**, and **world interaction** into a cohesive experience where every system feeds into another. Whether you are building large factories, exploring the world, or advancing through core mechanics, Harmony is designed to reward planning and cooperation.

This documentation serves as the **central reference** for all mechanics, systems, and content introduced by the modpack.

---

## What This Wiki Covers

You will find detailed documentation about the core systems of Create: Harmony, including:

- **Progression** – How players advance through phases and unlock new mechanics
- **Harmonic Core** – The central system driving progression and balance
- **Bestiary** – Creatures, entities, and progression-linked encounters
- **Currency & Economy** – Resource value, exchange systems, and automation incentives
- **Automation & Create Systems** – How Create mechanics integrate into Harmony
- **World & Exploration** – Structures, regions, and exploration-based progression
